User Manual

Introduction

UniPages is a social media site for students to socialise and collaborate with others students within their university, courses, groups and units.

Logging In/Creating a new user

Logging into UniPages requires a Google account, upon selecting Login you are redirected to Google to authenticate your account and if you already have a profile with UniPages you are automatically logged in and redirected to your home page. If you do not have a profile with us after authenticating with Google you are redirected to create a new profile.

Your homepage

Your homepage is a central hub for checking your posts, navigating to other pages and adding files to your repository. To get back to your home page at any time just click the UniPages logo in the top left corner.

Info Panel

On the right hand side you will see panel that contains information based upon which page you are visiting, these contains links to other related pages such as your course or university page from your profile information.

Posting + Commenting

You can post to your profile from the homepage and to specific profiles, your university, course, or groups from their respective pages. All posts have the ability to comment on them. You can also visit the pages of the those who are posting or commenting by selecting their names.

Repository

On the repository tab you will see two panels;

One that will include any current files you have in the repository, which are selectable and downloadable from the context bar below.

Another that is used to upload files or folders, you can drag multiple or single files/folders to the drop zone. These will be added to the table below and you can upload by selecting the upload button. 

NOTE: You can only upload 20 files at a time.

Profile Picture

You can upload profile pictures for your profile and any groups you own. This can be done by selecting upload picture on the info panel and you will be redirected to an upload form. Select a .JPG image to upload and select upload. If you then navigate back to the correct page the picture will now be the uploaded one.

Searching

You can find groups and profiles through the search page, you can navigate to this page by selecting search from the navigation bar. From here you can select whether you want to search for profiles or groups and search based upon name. You visit the page of any results by selecting the name of the profile/group.

Groups

You can create groups through the navigation bar and select from a number of options such as weather the group is a unit or not. This will change how the group is advertised and accessible. If you own a group you can change the profile picture as explained above.

Users cannot view a group's posts without joining that group, this can be done by visiting the group page and selecting join from the groups info panel. Similarly users can leave a group by selecting leave from the info panel

University

You can navigate to a university page by selecting it from a info panel, on this page you can create posts that will be seen by all users in your university.

Course

You can create a course from the navigation bar in a similar method to creating a group, this course will automatically become the user's course on creation.

NOTE: Unlike your profile and groups you cannot change the profile picture of your course

You can navigate to your course by selecting it from the info panel on your homepage. Here you can create posts that will be seen by all users on your course. You will also find a list of the courses units on the info panel

Other Users

You can find other users through clicking their names on a post/comment or by searching for them. On their profile page you will find information about what course they are on.

You can post on their page and those posts will be seen by that user and any user that visits that users page.
